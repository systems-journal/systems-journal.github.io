About

This is a webpage of a newly established Friendly Journal of Software Systems.

The main goal of this journal is to encourage people from industry to submit their work for a friendly peer-review and share with the community in one place.

Currently, there is no indexing of papers in scientific databases. Instead we just publish all accepted (peer-reviewed) papers on our website here.

We consider this journal to be just the first step to validate the idea and encourage the authors submit to indexed journals or conferences with proceedings later.

If you have any questions, please contact us by email systemsjournal@gmail.com.
